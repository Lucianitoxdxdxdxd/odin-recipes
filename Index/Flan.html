Honey flan

The honey flan is one of the best foods in the world and its small amount of sugar makes it typical of a meal for athletes who want to avoid adding sugar

Ingredients

  • 1 Liter of milk
  • 5 Egg yolks
  • 5 Whole eggs
  • 200 Grams of honey
  • 20 Grams of sugar
  • 5ml of vanila extract

Steps

  1. Making the caramel and letting it rest
  2. Mix the whole eggs and yolks with the honey
  3. When incorporated add milk
  4. Pour the mixture into the mold
